《开学第一课》观后感

爸爸的话看到了什么，领悟到了什么，从中学到了啥，以后要怎么做

九点的时候，我和爸爸妈妈到客厅看《开学第一课》。

在开学第一课上，我们看到了，吴京给我们拍的视频，还有柯洁讲了和alphago的围棋故事，郎朗和机器人特奥一起钢琴演奏，还有一个90多岁的老爷爷给我们讲了中国诗词的三美，还有京剧和中国诗词的结合。

通过观看开学第一课，我感受到了中国汉字的博大精深，和诗词的优美。我以后要好好学习汉字，正确做一个合格的中国人。

English summary

Grammar:

1. 可数名词的单复数：

1> 规则性名词，直接在名词后面加S，for examples:

   pencil->pencils     pen->pens    tree-> trees   cat->cats   dog->dogs

2> 以s，x， es 结尾的加es， for examples：

  bus-> buses    box-> boxes

3> 以f, fe结尾， 把f, fe 去掉加ves， for examples:

 leaf-> leaves   knife-> Knives

4>集合名词，没有单复数形式，但是都看成复数。For example:

 family, people

2. 系动词 的用法（is, are, am）for example

1> am 只和第一人称I 连用。

 I am a girl.

2>is 是用在第三人称单数的后面

  She/He/It is a girl/boy/dog.

3> are 是用在名词复数的后面 和第二名称

We/you/they are student.

3. 人称代词及所有格

第一人称 第二人称 第三人称
单数 I you she/he/it
宾格 me you she/him/it
所有格 my your her/his/its
复数 we you they
宾格 us you them
所有格 our your their

4， 人称替换（replace）for example:

Tom's mother is a cooker. -> She is a cooker

Peter's father is a teacher. -> He is a teacher.

The dog is lovely. -> It is lovely.

The girls are cute. -> They are cute.

5, 动词的现在进行时 for example:

1> 动词原形直接加ing

  fight-> fighting   climb-> climbing   sleep->sleeping drink-drinking    eat-eating

2> 双写后面的辅音字母再加ing
run->running swim-> swimming

3> 以 'e'结尾的去 e再加ing

   dance-> dancing

6, a, an 和the的区别与用法

1> a 和an 只用于单数名词/形容词前面，但是an 用于名词/形容词的第一个字母是元音:

a cat                    a cute cat       an apple/elephant/orange

2> the用于特指

the cat/dog 这只猫/狗

7, 一个句子的第一个单词的首字母要大写，人名字的第一个字母要大写

I am a woman. This is Peter Wang.

Sentences：

    There is only one elephant/zebra.

    There are two/there... elephants/zebras.

    There are zero elephants/zebras.

  2. what is this/that? It is a dog.

  3. what are these/those? They are turtles.

  15. How are you? I am fine/happy/angry/hungry/tired...

  16. How old are you? I am six years old.

  17. What is the weather like today? It is sunny/cloudy/rainy...

  18. What day is it today?

    It is Monday/Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday/Friday/Saturday/Sunday?

  19. What month is it now?

    It is January/February/March/April/May/June/July/August/September/October
    /November/December

  20. What season is it now? It is Spring/Summer/Autumn/Winter.
